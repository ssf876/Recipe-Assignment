Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of Oats
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients together
  2. Use spray oil to lightly coat pan/griddle
  3. On heat setting 5, pour in a portion of the mixture
  4. Cook for 2 mins on each side, repeat until mixture is finished.
  5. Enjoy!
